Our Applications

PollyTimer Free tier: no data collected Premium: cloud features below

PollyTimer is a voice-controlled timer app for iOS and Android. It lets you set, pause, and cancel timers using natural speech, and receive spoken countdowns and alerts.

Free Tier — No Data Collected

In its free configuration, PollyTimer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information. All features described in this section operate entirely on your device.

  • Voice & Speech: Processed by your device’s built-in OS speech recognition (Apple or Android). We never receive your audio.
  • Timer data: Stored locally on your device only.
  • Notifications: Generated locally. No notification data is sent to our servers.
  • Text-to-speech: Uses your device’s on-device TTS engine. No audio leaves your device.

Premium Tier — Cloud Features

Premium subscribers may optionally enable cloud-based features. These features are opt-in only and require account creation. The following data is processed when enabled:

Feature Data sent Where processed Retained?
Cloud-enhanced voice recognition Transcribed text of your timer request (not raw audio). Sent only when on-device confidence is low (<85%). Supabase Edge Function (hosted in the US) → Anthropic API for parsing intent. Text is not stored after the response is returned. No — processed in-transit only
Cloud sync Timer group names, timer labels, durations, and tags. No voice recordings. Supabase database (hosted in the US), linked to your account via Row Level Security. Only you can access your data. Yes — until you delete your account or data
Account authentication Email address and hashed password. Supabase Auth (hosted in the US). Yes — until account deletion

Analytics

We use PostHog for product analytics. PostHog collects anonymized usage events (e.g. “timer started,” “group saved”) to help us improve the app. No personally identifiable information is attached to these events. Analytics can be disabled in Settings → Privacy.

Data minimization

Cloud-enhanced voice recognition sends only the text transcript of your voice input — never raw audio. The transcript is used for a single parsing request and is not logged or retained after the response is returned.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may be distributed through third-party platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play, Amazon Appstore). Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies.

Premium cloud features use the following sub-processors:

  • Supabase (database and authentication) — supabase.com/privacy
  • Anthropic (natural language parsing, cloud NLP only) — anthropic.com/privacy
  • PostHog (analytics) — posthog.com/privacy

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

Free-tier data is stored only on your device, under your operating system’s security controls. Premium cloud data is protected by Supabase’s Row Level Security policies — only authenticated users can access their own records. All data in transit uses TLS encryption.

Data Retention and Deletion

Free-tier data persists only on your device. To delete it, uninstall the app or clear app data in your device settings.

Premium cloud data is retained until you delete your account. To request deletion, email us at the address below or use Settings → Account → Delete Account within the app.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to access personal data we hold about you
  • The right to request correction or deletion of your data
  • The right to opt out of analytics (Settings → Privacy → Analytics)
  • The right to opt out of cloud NLP (Settings → Privacy → Cloud voice recognition)
  • The right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority
