Our Applications

PollyTimer No data collected

PollyTimer is a voice-controlled timer app for iOS and Android. It lets you set, pause, and cancel timers using natural speech, and receive spoken countdowns and alerts.

What We Collect

In its current release, PollyTimer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.

Voice & Speech

Voice input is processed entirely by your device’s built-in operating system speech recognition (Apple Speech Recognition on iOS, Android Speech Recognition on Android). We never receive, store, or transmit your voice recordings or transcriptions.

Timer Data

All timer configurations and history are stored locally on your device using standard device storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Notifications

PollyTimer uses local notifications to alert you when timers complete. These are generated entirely on your device — no notification data is sent to our servers.

Text-to-Speech

Spoken countdown announcements use your device’s built-in text-to-speech engine. No audio data is transmitted externally.

Future Updates

If a future version of PollyTimer introduces account creation, cloud sync, or analytics, this policy will be updated before those features are released, and you will be notified within the app.

Third-Party Services

Our apps may be distributed through third-party platforms (Apple App Store, Google Play, Amazon Appstore). Those platforms operate under their own privacy policies, which govern any data they collect as part of the download and billing process.

Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

Data Security

Because our current apps store all data locally on your device, your information remains under the security controls provided by your operating system. We encourage you to keep your device software up to date and use a secure lock screen.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • The right to know what personal data we hold about you

Since our current apps collect no personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. Future versions that collect data will include in-app mechanisms to exercise these rights.
